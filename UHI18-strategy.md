## 🪝 The "Winner" Strategy: Yield-Compounding Stablecoin Manager
This project will bridge **ForgeX Vaults** with **Uniswap v4 Pools**.

### The Concept
Build a hook that allows Uniswap v4 pools to hold **ForgeX Vault shares** instead of idle underlying assets.
- **Problem**: In standard AMMs, liquidity is "lazy" and only earns fees when someone swaps.
- **Solution**: A **"Yield-Compounding" hook** that moves idle pool liquidity into ForgeX ERC-4626 vaults, earning **Stablecoin Yield** on top of **Trading Fees**.

### Key Features
- **Yield Distribution**: Use the `afterSwap` hook to harvest interest and donate it back to active Liquidity Providers (LPs).
- **Dynamic Fee Adjustment**: Automatically adjust pool fees based on the specific yield-bearing profile of the vault-wrapped assets.
- **African Savings Angle**: Market this as the ultimate tool for African users (PiggyVest model) to get the best-possible yield on their USD savings.
